About

ValuNest is a metaphor that came from two (2) words, Value and Nest, where Value refers to the Price and Nest refers to the home of birds.

Background of the Application

ValuNest uses machine learning and comprehensive datasets that predicts the price of a house base on its Plot Area, Number of Rooms (Bedroom and Bathroom), and Number off Stories.
